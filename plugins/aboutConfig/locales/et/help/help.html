About:Config

This page provides technical information about the configuration of the current blog (as well as configuration common to all the blogs). These parameters are usually managed by other pages in the administration interface.
Only modify something here if you know what you are doing!

Mõnda parameetrit saab muuta ainult sellel leheküljel.

Leheküljel on kaks vahekaarti: Praeguse blogi seaded (blogi nimi) ja Üldised seaded, et hallata vastavalt praeguseid blogi seadeid ja üldisi seadeid.

Erinevatele leheküljeosadele pääseb otse juurde, kasutades rippmenüüt Goto.
